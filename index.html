What is HackNUthon?

 

Rapidly rising Innovation and Entrepreneurship in India made us organize the HackNUThon 2020, a stage to discover technologies, innovate ideas and solve various challenges. A non-stop, 36 hours long, a challenging product development competition, where participants can come up with their problem statements or they will be given the problem statements for innovative solutions to the respective problems. Prizes upto Rs.30,000 for winners and get cool goodies from our partners to all participants! Its gonna be an real tough challenge! Can you beat through it?
