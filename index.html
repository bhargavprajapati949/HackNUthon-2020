What is HackNUthon?

 

As innovation and entrepreneurship are at the peak in India, we, at Nirma University, are organizing HackNUthon 2020, a platform for identifying new and disruptive technologies and innovations to solve different challenges. It's a non-stop 36 hours product development competition, where problem statements will be posed to participants or participants can come up with their own problem statements for innovative solutions.
