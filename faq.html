25th September, 2020 (tentatively)
Can I come up with my own problem statement?
Yes. We encourage you to come up with your own problem statements provided they fall under the given themes.
Who can attend?
Anyone with an idea, interested in learning new technologies or interested in developing a new idea in general.

Maximum team size of 4 and a minimum of 2.

Not necessary.
